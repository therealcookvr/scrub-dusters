About Scrub Dusters

Scrub Dusters is a small car washing service focused on doing a careful, thorough job with clear, simple pricing.

The aim is straightforward: turn up when agreed, do the work properly, and leave the car looking clean without any confusion about cost.

Bookings are handled online so you can quickly choose a day, time and wash type that works for you.
