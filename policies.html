Policies

How Scrub Dusters works.

Bookings & cancellations

  • Bookings can be changed or cancelled by contacting the phone number or email provided.
  • Please give as much notice as possible if you need to cancel.

Products & shipping

  • Products bought over £10 may include free delivery (local only, subject to agreement).
  • Basket totals over £30 qualify for free shipping where available.

Loyalty & warranty

  • Loyalty card: £5 per month, gives 10% discount on washes and products.
  • Any “warranty” or re‑clean offers are agreed individually and valid for one month from the wash date.
